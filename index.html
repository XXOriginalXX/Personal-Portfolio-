About Me

Hi, I’m Adithyan, a passionate Computer Science student specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I’ve got hands-on experience working with Python and various machine learning techniques. But when it comes to data-driven solutions and building powerful AI models, that’s where I thrive! I'm always on the lookout for new challenges to learn and grow in the world of AI and ML.
